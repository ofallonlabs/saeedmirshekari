Features

This course is designed to help (1) the beginners, enthusiasts, and non-experts in this field who are willing to learn more about data science and (2) those professionals in other fields who have started thinking about starting a career in this growing job market.

Our Mission

We help you to become a Data Scientist fast. The global demand for new Data Scientists in industry is up on the roof and we are committed to help closing this gap by training more Data Scientists.

Our Plan

We take you through a hands-on experience end-to-end. By the end of this course you will be able to think like a data-scientist, ask the right questions, and take the right tools and approaches towards the best solutions.

Our Vision

Everybody can be a Data Scientist. During this course, no prior experience in advance-math or computer programming is assumed. However, the more you know in advance, the faster/more you will learn in this course.

Our Care

We have your back 24/7. We not only help you through the end-to-end process, but also try to understand your background and your goals to be able to serve you the best towards your desired results. We are here for you.

Why Joining This Course?

Giving Perspective

Lots of people lose the big-picture of their learning journey by focusing on too much of details. This course covers all the bases of the Data-Science field as a whole through an end-to-end real-world project in a short period of time.

Hands-on Experience

Knowing the theory is just not enough in today's job market. This course offers focused assignments and quizzes for the students on each topic to make sure they enjoy a hands-on experience through their learning journey.

Live Interactions

The sense of community and having somebody alongside you is a huge part of every memorable journey. This course is designed to provide an interactive learning environment through one-to-one calls and weekly live office hours.

Career Service

This course not only provides unique learning resources but also offer career services such as one-to-one resume-review sessions and Ask-Me-Anything sessions by top experts who have already advanced in their journey.

Learning Path

Overview

The course is divided into 5 sections (week 1-5) and 14 separate modules. The course materials each week are focused on a certain topic with a specific practical goal.

The course materials each week come in three types: (1) lectures, followed by (2) assignments, and (3) quizzes. At the end of each week, there will be a 1-hour Q&A session with the instructor in which you can ask questions and interact with the class live.

Metaphorically speaking, in this course we will take you to a beautiful lake for a joyful snorkeling session. Bring yourself and enjoy your journey as you learn practical lessons about the lake and snorkeling!
