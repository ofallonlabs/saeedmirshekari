# FIFA World Cup 2026 Group L Predictions

## Market Odds vs Model-X Forecasts

**Published Before Kickoff — June 11, 2026**

This article is part of our World Cup 2026 prediction series comparing betting-market expectations against Model-X forecasts.

The market odds represent the average of FanDuel, DraftKings, BetMGM, OddsChecker, and Bet365. Model-X is an AI-powered football forecasting model trained on historical international match data and Transfermarkt-style squad valuation information.

The goal is simple: publish every forecast before kickoff and revisit the results after the group stage concludes to evaluate both the market and Model-X against reality.

This article focuses on **Group L**.

---

# Group L Overview

### Teams

* 🇬🇧 England
* 🇭🇷 Croatia
* 🇬🇭 Ghana
* 🇵🇦 Panama

Group L features one of the strongest favorites in the tournament alongside a fascinating race for second place.

England enters as the clear favorite and is projected to top the group comfortably. Croatia is expected to challenge for qualification, while Ghana and Panama look to disrupt the standings.

The betting market and Model-X agree on the two teams expected to advance, but they disagree on one key match that could determine how comfortable Croatia's path becomes.
