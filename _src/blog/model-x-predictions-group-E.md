# FIFA World Cup 2026 Group E Predictions

## Market Odds vs Model-X Forecasts

**Published Before Kickoff — June 11, 2026**

The FIFA World Cup 2026 will bring together 48 nations across the United States, Canada, and Mexico in the largest tournament in football history.

Before the first match is played, we are publishing a complete set of group-stage forecasts using two independent prediction sources:

1. **Market Consensus** — average odds from FanDuel, DraftKings, BetMGM, OddsChecker, and Bet365.
2. **Model-X** — an AI-powered football forecasting model trained on historical international football data and Transfermarkt-style squad valuation information.

The goal is simple. Publish predictions before kickoff and then revisit the results after the group stage to evaluate both the betting market and Model-X against reality.

This article focuses on **Group E**.

---

# Group E Overview

### Teams

* 🇩🇪 Germany
* 🇨🇼 Curaçao
* 🇨🇮 Ivory Coast
* 🇪🇨 Ecuador

Group E appears to have a clear favorite.

Germany enters the tournament as one of the strongest teams in the competition and is expected to control the group. The real battle is projected to be between Ecuador and Ivory Coast for the second qualification position.

Curaçao enters as the underdog but will have opportunities to influence the race for qualification.

Unlike Group A, where the market and Model-X produced dramatically different outcomes, Group E shows remarkable agreement.

# Final Thoughts

Group E is one of the clearest groups in the tournament.
