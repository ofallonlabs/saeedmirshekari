**Published Before Kickoff — June 11, 2026**

This article is part of our World Cup 2026 prediction series comparing betting-market expectations against Model-X forecasts. Model-X is an AI-powered football forecasting model trained on historical international match data and Transfermarkt-style squad information.

The purpose of this series is simple: publish predictions before kickoff and evaluate them after the group stage concludes.
