# FIFA World Cup 2026 Group K Predictions

## Market Odds vs Model-X Forecasts

**Published Before Kickoff — June 11, 2026**

This article is part of our World Cup 2026 prediction series comparing betting-market expectations against Model-X forecasts.

The market odds represent the average of FanDuel, DraftKings, BetMGM, OddsChecker, and Bet365. Model-X is an AI-powered football forecasting model trained on historical international match data and Transfermarkt-style squad valuation information.

The objective is simple: publish predictions before kickoff and evaluate both forecasting approaches after the group stage concludes.

---

# Group K Overview

### Teams

* 🇵🇹 Portugal
* 🇨🇩 DR Congo
* 🇺🇿 Uzbekistan
* 🇨🇴 Colombia

Group K features a clear hierarchy according to both forecasting systems.

Portugal enters as the strongest team in the group and is expected to finish first. Colombia is projected to secure the second qualification spot, while DR Congo and Uzbekistan are expected to battle for third place.

Unlike Groups F, G, H, and I, there is very little disagreement between the betting market and Model-X in Group K.
