# FIFA World Cup 2026 Group H Predictions

## Market Odds vs Model-X Forecasts

**Published Before Kickoff — June 11, 2026**

The FIFA World Cup 2026 will be hosted across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Before the tournament begins, we are publishing a complete set of group-stage forecasts comparing:

1. **Market Consensus** — average odds from FanDuel, DraftKings, BetMGM, OddsChecker, and Bet365.
2. **Model-X** — an AI-powered football forecasting model trained on historical international football data and Transfermarkt-style squad valuation information.

The objective is simple: publish predictions before kickoff and revisit them after the group stage concludes to compare the market and Model-X against reality.

This article focuses on **Group H**.

# Group H Overview

### Teams

* 🇪🇸 Spain
